Vegetarian Fat Loss Plan

Goal Setup

  • Target Weight: 62 kg
  • Timeline: 3–4 months
  • Calories: ~1300–1400 kcal/day
  • Protein: 60–70 g/day

Diet Plan

Morning

  • Warm water + lemon
  • 4–5 soaked almonds + 1 walnut
  • Black coffee or green tea

Breakfast

  • 2 Besan chilla + curd OR 1 bowl poha with veggies
  • 1 bowl oats + milk + nuts (no sugar)

Lunch

  • 1 cup dal or chole/rajma
  • 1 cup sabzi (bhindi, lauki, paneer, etc.)
  • 1 roti (no ghee) OR ½ cup rice
  • Salad + lemon

Snack

  • Black coffee / green tea / buttermilk
  • 1 fruit (no banana/mango)
  • Handful of roasted chana/peanuts

Dinner

  • 1 bowl soup + paneer/tofu stir-fry OR 1 roti + sabzi

Before Bed

  • 1 glass warm turmeric milk (optional)
