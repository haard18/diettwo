Cut 10kg — Simple Veg Diet & Home Workout

For a 21-year-old female, 72 kg. No exotic food. Home-friendly exercises. Conservative, sustainable plan (12–16 weeks).

Plan summary Calories ~1300–1400 · Protein 60–70g · Timeline 3–4 months

Daily routine (simple)

  • Wake: warm water + lemon, 4–5 soaked almonds + 1 walnut
  • Breakfast 8–9am, Lunch 1–2pm, Snack 4–5pm, Dinner 7–8pm
  • Drink ≥3L water, sleep 7–8 hours, weigh weekly same time
  • 1 cheat meal/week, not cheat day
Meal options (pick 1 per slot) Ordinary Indian veg food. Swap similar items freely.
Breakfast
  • 2 besan chilla + curd
  • OR 1 small bowl poha with veggies
  • OR 1 bowl oats in milk (no sugar) + nuts
Lunch
  • 1 cup dal/rajma/chole (moderate oil)
  • 1 cup sabzi (bhindi, lauki, methi, paneer)
  • 1 roti OR 1/2 cup rice + salad
Snack
  • Buttermilk / green tea + 1 fruit (apple/guava/papaya)
  • Optional: handful roasted chana / peanuts
Dinner
  • 1 bowl soup + paneer/tofu stir-fry
  • OR 1 roti + sabzi (avoid rice at dinner)

Protein sources: paneer, tofu, dals, sprouts, milk, curd. Use mustard/olive oil modestly.

What to avoid Simple rules beat willpower.
  • No sugary drinks, chips, biscuits, fried food, refined white bread
  • No late-night heavy meals; drink water if "hungry" at night
  • Limit bananas/mangoes while cutting (higher sugar)
Weekly strategy Consistency > perfection
  • Workout 45 min/day, 5–6 days/week. Rest 1 day.
  • Track weight weekly, progress photos monthly.
  • If energy low: add 100–150 kcal from nuts/dairy; do not starve.
Simple substitutions Practical swaps
  • Replace white rice with 3/4 cup cooked rice or extra roti.
  • Swap sweets for fruit + Greek/yogurt (unsweetened).
  • Use roasted chana instead of namkeen for crunch.

This is a practical, normal-food plan. Stick to it and don't overcomplicate.

Quick 7-day rotating sample (pick similar swaps)
  1. Mon: Besan chilla / Dal, roti / Dinner soup + tofu / Workout A
  2. Tue: Oats / Rajma, sabzi / Dinner roti + sabzi / Workout B
  3. Wed: Poha / Chole, rice(½ cup) / Dinner paneer stir fry / Active rest (walk)
  4. Thu: Besan chilla / Dal + roti / Dinner soup / Workout A
  5. Fri: Oats / Rajma + salad / Dinner roti + sabzi / Workout B

  7. Sun: Rest or light yoga / meal choices as above
Tips
  • First response to hunger — drink water.
  • Protein each meal to preserve muscle.
  • Be patient — 0.5–1 kg weekly is sustainable.
Advanced notes (click)

If you want to progress: increase protein to 1.2–1.6g/kg of target bodyweight, add resistance (dumbbells or filled water bottles), and slowly reintroduce slightly higher calories as maintenance once at goal.
